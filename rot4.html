Rotation 4: Laser Cutting and Engraving and Vinyl Cutting



Project Description:


The first project of this cycle was to create a laser cut box with both rastering and engraving. I made my box in corel draw a three by three inch box on cardboard. This project was my second time using the laser cutter.







The second project of this cycle was designing three different shapes that when laser printed will connect together. This project I also designed in Corel Draw.






The third project of this cycle was desiging and laser printing on anything we wanted. I chose a glass. I made two glasses, both with images from google on corel. The dolphin cup turned out better than the pineapple cup because it was a clearer image.
