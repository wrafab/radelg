Group Design Challenges:



On the first day we were put into groups to compelete several different challenges. We were given a bag of supplies and we were only allowed to use what was in the bag, plus tape, to meet the requirements. One of the challenges was to try and make the longest ramp for a marble to roll down, but the marble had to land in a square. We made a long ramp, but we did not support it in the best spots, so the marble ended up falling off of the ramp. We tested the ramp before the "real" round, and the marble sucessfully went down the ramp, and lended in the square. I helped create border of straws around the square to ensure the marble would stay inside of the box.



Our next challenge was to use raw spaghetti to support a marshmellow. The goal was to have the higest structure, without it toppling over. Ours did not fall over, but it was not the highest. We had a tripod design, but we were not successful in adding height to the design. This was a more difficult challenge and a little bit frustrating since my ideas were kind of just shoved aside. I helped by ripping pieces of tape and holding sticks of raw pasta together.
